The platform

Six sections. One feed. All signal.

Every part of FloMKa is built around one principle — give you real data, not marketing copy. Here's what's inside.

01 · Tools

AI Tools Directory

Every major AI tool ranked by a composite score built from community votes, complaint weight, and week-on-week momentum. Filter by category — Coding, Search, Writing, Video, Inference. Search by use case. Compare any two tools side by side. No paid rankings, no sponsored positions — the score is always honest.

02 · Complaints

Real User Complaints Board

We track what real users are writing on Reddit, HackerNews, and developer forums — sorted by upvote count so the biggest frustrations always surface first. See exactly what's broken with any AI tool before you commit to it, build on top of it, or recommend it to your team. Every complaint is a verified data point.

03 · Ideas

Startup Ideas Radar

Every idea is reverse-engineered from a real, high-volume complaint. Each entry shows you the problem, the startup opportunity, the evidence behind it (Reddit upvotes, HN comments, forum threads), and a confidence score. You get the validated insight — not the brainstorm. The best startup ideas come from the most-upvoted user pain.

04 · Brief

Executive Brief

A daily AI-generated intelligence report personalised to your industry — Technology, Fintech, Healthcare, E-commerce, Manufacturing and more. Four sections every day: Risk Radar, Adoption Signal, Opportunity Map, and one Monday Action Item. Powered by live FloMKa data and Groq's llama-3.3-70b. Forward it to your team in one click.

05 · Trends

Trends Dashboard

Six-week momentum charts for every major AI tool — so you see what's rising fast, what's plateauing, and what's in decline. Category-level momentum scores show you macro shifts weeks before they become obvious. Are Autonomous Agents rising? Is AI video accelerating? The trends dashboard answers it with data, not opinion.

06 · Finder

AI Tool Finder

Describe exactly what you need to do — "generate SQL from plain English for my non-technical team", "summarise long PDFs for our legal department" — and get honest, complaint-aware recommendations in seconds. Not just what works, but what to watch out for. Powered by real FloMKa complaint data, not generic AI knowledge.
